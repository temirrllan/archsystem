Nyutonnyng 2-shi zanyn tusindirshi
Nyutonnyng 2-shi zany — kush, massa men udeu arasyndagy baylanysty anyqtaydy:

F = m · a
Munda:
• F — kush (Nyuton, N)
• m — dene massasy (kg)
• a — udeu (m/s²)

Mysaly: massasy 5 kg dene 3 m/s² udeumen qozgalsa, kush:
F = 5 · 3 = 15 N
Al gravitatsiya kushinin formulasy?
Gravitatsiya kushy formulasy:
F = G · (m₁ · m₂) / r²
G = 6.674 × 10⁻¹¹ N·m²/kg²
